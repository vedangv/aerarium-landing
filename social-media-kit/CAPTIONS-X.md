# X / Twitter Posts

**Why X (FinTwit):** finance Twitter is where investors argue about exposure,
the Fed, and 13F filings all day. Your hook is *tailor-made* for it. Reach comes
from the hook and replies, not hashtags — use 0–1 tags.

**Format notes:**
- 280 chars per tweet. The first tweet must stand alone as a hook.
- Threads work great for teaching, then revealing the product in the last tweet.
- Put the link in the **last** tweet of a thread, not the first (first-tweet
  links suppress reach).
- Use X UTM links from `LINKS.csv`.

When you create an X account, start with these.

## X cadence & etiquette
- 1–2 posts/day is sustainable; reply to others' FinTwit threads to build reach.
- Quote-tweet relevant exposure/concentration discussions with your X-Ray angle.
- Don't spam the link. Earn the click with the insight first.
