# Launch-day graphic — spec

The one asset the launch kit references but doesn't have yet (the launch-day IG
post + Story currently fall back to a screenshot). Build this in Canva/Figma —
it's a layout + copy brief, not a design you need to invent.

Reel Script 3 (founder video) and the launch-day caption already exist
(`REEL-SCRIPTS.md`, `CAPTIONS-INSTAGRAM.md`) and are on-message — this just gives
them a matching hero image.

## Formats
- **1080×1080** (IG feed post).
- **1080×1920** (IG Story) — same elements, stacked taller.

## Brand (match the landing)
- Background: near-black emerald (`#080b09`), subtle emerald radial glow top-center.
- Headline font: a serif (Instrument Serif / similar) — matches the site's
  `font-editorial`.
- Accent: emerald `#10b981`. Body: light slate `#cbd5e1`.
- Logo: the gold "A" mark, top-center or bottom.

## Copy (pick one headline)
**Option A — the hook (recommended):**
> It's live.
> *See what you actually own.*
> Aerarium · free on the App Store

**Option B — the question:**
> How much NVDA do you *really* own —
> once you look through your ETFs?
> Find out → Aerarium, now on the App Store

**Option C — plain:**
> Aerarium is live.
> Portfolio discipline, in your pocket.
> Free on the App Store

Footer line (all options): `Read-only · monitoring, not trading`

## Layout
- Headline centered, large serif, emerald accent on the second line.
- Optional: a clean phone screenshot of the X-Ray screen
  (`portfolio_4_portfolio-xray.png`) angled bottom-right, or keep it pure-type
  (type-only reads more premium and is faster to make).
- Logo + "Free on the App Store" lockup at the bottom.
- Story variant: add a "Link in bio ↓" / swipe-up cue.

## Hard rules (YMYL — do not break)
- No returns/performance claims, no "beat the market," no Bloomberg/terminal
  comparison. Keep "read-only / monitoring, not trading."
- This is the **Portfolio app** launch — keep it about the app + the X-Ray hook.
  (Research has its own cyan visual identity; don't mix them here.)

## Where it's used
- Launch-day IG feed post (replaces the `portfolio_1_overview.png` fallback in
  `CAPTIONS-INSTAGRAM.md` → "Launch-day post").
- Launch-day Story.
- Save the file as `social-media-kit/launch_graphic.png` so the calendar can
  reference it.
