# LinkedIn Posts

**Why LinkedIn matters for Aerarium:** your audience — disciplined, rules-based
investors, finance professionals, CFAs — lives here more than anywhere. LinkedIn
also rewards genuine founder narrative and long-form. This is likely your
**highest-quality** channel even if not your highest-volume.

**Format notes:**
- First 2 lines show before "…see more." Make them a hook.
- Line breaks (one idea per line) beat dense paragraphs.
- 1–3 hashtags max, at the very end.
- Links in comments sometimes get more reach than in-post, but in-post is fine —
  test both. Use the LinkedIn UTM links from `LINKS.csv`.
- Post when you'll be free to reply for an hour (early weekday mornings work well).

When you create a LinkedIn account, make these your first posts.

### L2 — The hidden-exposure insight (teaches, then reveals)

```
Most investors underestimate their single-name exposure by 2x or more.

Here's why.

You hold a few funds for diversification — say SPY and QQQ. You also hold a
little NVDA directly because you have conviction.

On your brokerage screen, NVDA looks like a small slice. Maybe 8%.

But NVDA is one of the largest weights in both SPY and QQQ. So your *real* NVDA
exposure is your direct shares PLUS its weight inside every fund you hold —
across every account.

That 8% can quietly be 18%+.

You can't manage risk you can't see. And you definitely can't follow your own
investing rules if your true exposure is hidden inside fund wrappers.

This is the problem I built Aerarium to solve: ETF look-through that rolls your
real exposure up across accounts, so your rules apply to reality instead of a
stale spreadsheet.

It's free in beta. Link in comments.

#Investing #Fintech #PortfolioManagement
```
