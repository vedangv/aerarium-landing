## Engagement tips (Instagram-specific)
- **Reels reach 5–10x what static posts do** right now. Lead with Reels (see
  `REEL-SCRIPTS.md`); use static posts for depth.
- Put hashtags in the **first comment** if you prefer a clean caption — reach is
  the same.
- Reply to every comment in the first hour; it boosts distribution.
- Use **Stories** to reshare each post with a "link sticker" straight to the
  founder list — Stories link stickers are clickable (your bio link isn't, in-feed).
- Ask one question at the end of Stories ("Guess your real NVDA exposure 👀") —
  replies train the algorithm that people care.
