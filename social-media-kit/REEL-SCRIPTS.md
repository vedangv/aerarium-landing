# Reel / Video Scripts

Reels are the single highest-reach format on Instagram in 2026 — they out-reach
static posts roughly 5–10x and are shown to non-followers. For a launch, this is
your best free distribution. The same clips work for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and
X video.

You don't need fancy production. A screen recording of the app + text captions +
your voice (or trending-but-quiet audio) is enough. Below are shot-by-shot
scripts a non-videographer can follow.

**Universal rules:**
- **Hook in the first 1.5 seconds** or people scroll past. Open on the surprise,
  not a logo.
- Keep it **15–30 seconds.** Shorter finishes; finishing = reach.
- **Add on-screen captions** (most people watch muted).
- Vertical 9:16. Film the phone screen recording at full resolution.
- One call-to-action at the end, calm: "Free in beta. Link in bio."
- Don't say any banned phrase (see `MESSAGING.md`).

## 🎬 Script 1 — "The 8% that's actually 18%" (your flagship)

**Goal:** the hidden-exposure reveal. This is THE one. Post first, pin it.

| Time | On screen | Caption text (overlay) | Voice / note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0.0–2.0s | Brokerage-style screen showing "NVDA 8%" | **"You think you own 8% NVDA."** | Hook. Start mid-action, no intro. |
| 2.0–5.0s | Tap into Aerarium Portfolio X-Ray; donut starts expanding | "But your ETFs hold it too…" | "SPY holds it. QQQ holds it." |
| 5.0–9.0s | X-Ray donut resolves; number ticks 8% → 18.7% | **"Actual exposure: 18.7%"** | "Across every account, looked through every fund." |
| 9.0–13.0s | Show the rolled-up exposure list | "Your real exposure was hiding inside your funds." | calm, matter-of-fact |
| 13.0–16.0s | Aerarium logo + "Read-only. Never trades." | **"See what you actually own. Free in beta → link in bio"** | one CTA |

**Caption:** use `CAPTIONS-INSTAGRAM.md` A1.
**Asset source:** screen-record the real X-Ray flow, or animate
`portfolio_4_portfolio-xray.png`.

## 🎬 Script 3 — Founder / launch-day video (you on camera)

**Goal:** the human moment. Film yourself talking to the camera, 20–30s.

**Script (say naturally, don't read robotically):**
```
Hey — I'm Vedang, I'm a CFA, and I built this thing called Aerarium.

For years I ran my own portfolio from a spreadsheet. I knew my rules, I tracked
my allocation… but I could never actually answer one question:

how much of any single stock do I REALLY own — once you look through my ETFs and
across all my accounts?

So I built the tool that answers it. It shows your true exposure, and tells you
when your portfolio drifts from your own rules.

It's read-only — it never makes trades. And it's live on the App Store today,
free to start.

If that sounds like how you think about investing, the link's in my bio. Thanks.
```

**On-screen captions:** burn in subtitles. End card: logo + "Free on the App
Store • Link in bio."
**Caption:** `CAPTIONS-INSTAGRAM.md` launch-day post.

## Production shortcuts for a solo founder
- **Screen recording:** iOS built-in screen recorder captures the app cleanly.
- **Captions/text:** CapCut (free) auto-captions and has the text styles people
  expect on Reels. Or Instagram's native caption sticker.
- **Audio:** pick a *quiet, trending* audio in Instagram's library (using trending
  audio boosts reach), keep your voice or captions primary.
- **Batch it:** film 3–4 Reels in one sitting, schedule them out. Sustainable.
- **Reuse:** every Reel re-posts to TikTok, Shorts, and X with zero changes. One
  film session = 4 platforms.
