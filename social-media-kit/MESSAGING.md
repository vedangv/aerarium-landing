# Messaging Foundation

This is the source of truth for *what Aerarium says and how it sounds*. When you
write your own posts, comments, or replies, check them against this page. If a
sentence breaks a rule here, rewrite it.

---

## Positioning (memorize the first line)

**One-liner:**
> Aerarium is the discipline layer between market research and your real portfolio.

**Plain-English version (for normal people):**
> Aerarium shows you what you *actually* own — looking through your ETFs and
> across all your accounts — and tells you when your portfolio has drifted from
> your own rules.

**What it is NOT** (say these out loud when someone misunderstands):
- Not a brokerage or trading app — Aerarium never places trades.
- Not a robo-advisor — it doesn't tell you what to buy.
- Not stock tips — it shows you *your* data, not predictions.
- Not a Bloomberg terminal — Research is a source-first workspace, not a terminal.

---

## The hook (lead with this almost everywhere)

> **Illustrative example: you think you own 8% NVDA. Look through your ETFs —
> it's actually 18.7%.**

Why it works: it creates a knowledge gap ("wait, is that true for me?"), it's
specific and credible, and it sets up Portfolio X-Ray and Policy Score as the
payoff. Variations you can rotate:

- "Your portfolio says 8% NVDA. Your ETFs say otherwise."
- "How much NVDA do you *actually* own? Your brokerage screen may not show the full answer."
- "SPY + QQQ + your direct NVDA = way more NVDA than you think."
- "You can't follow your own investing rules if you can't see your real exposure."

## Voice & tone

Aerarium sounds like **a disciplined CFA who respects your intelligence** — calm,
precise, a little dry, never hype. Think Luffu/Poly calm, not crypto-bro loud.

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Concrete numbers ("18.7%", "Policy Score 94") | Vague hype ("amazing", "game-changer") |
| Short, declarative sentences | Stacked buzzwords |
| Honest founder voice ("I built this because…") | Corporate "we are thrilled to announce" |
| Explain the *why* | Demand the click |
| 1 idea per post | 5 features crammed in |
| Respect ("here's your data") | Pressure ("you NEED this") |

Emoji: sparingly on Instagram (✨📊🔍 ok), almost never on LinkedIn, never on
Reddit. One per post max as a rule of thumb.

## ⚠️ Compliance: the lines you must not cross

Aerarium is a **finance product (YMYL)**. Crossing these can get posts removed,
the App Store listing flagged, or worse. Non-negotiable.

### Banned words/phrases (never use)
- "guaranteed", "guaranteed returns", "risk-free"
- "beat the market", "outperform", "alpha" (as a promise)
- "best stocks", "stocks to buy", "hot picks", "AI stock picks"
- "get rich", "build wealth fast", "10x your money"
- "you should buy/sell", "we recommend buying", any direct advice
- "bank-grade security", "military-grade" (unless you can prove the exact standard)
- "trusted by thousands", any user count or testimonial you can't verify
- "financial advice" framing of any kind

### Always-safe framing (use freely)
- "See your *real* exposure" (showing their data, not advising)
- "Your rules, made visible"
- "Read-only. Aerarium never places trades." ← use constantly
- "Monitoring, not trading"
- "Source-first research" (for Research product)
- "Free during beta"
- "Built by a CFA charterholder" (true credential, fine to state)

### The safe-harbor instinct
If a sentence tells the reader **what to do with money**, it's wrong. If it shows
them **what is true about their own money**, it's fine. Aerarium is a mirror, not
a megaphone.

## Proof points (your credibility, ranked)

Use these to back up the hook. Lead with #1–2; they're strongest.

1. **Portfolio X-Ray** — ETF look-through reveals true stock/sector/currency
   exposure across all accounts. *This is the demo that sells the product.*
2. **Policy Score** — one transparent score for whether your portfolio still
   matches your written rules (drift, concentration, liquidity, goals, cadence).
3. **IPS Cockpit** — turn a written investment policy into live guardrails.
4. **Read-only + private** — SnapTrade read-only sync, encrypted fields, export
   & delete controls, no trades ever placed.
5. **Founder credibility** — solo founder, CFA charterholder, built from a real
   personal portfolio problem.
6. **Research** — 13F overlap, macro dashboard, Fed curve, revenue segments,
   ownership/insiders — source-first, public-data.

## Founder story (your secret weapon)

A solo-founder beta launch runs on the *person*, not the brand. Your story, in
one paragraph you can reuse anywhere:

> I'm a CFA charterholder. I tracked my own portfolio in spreadsheets for years —
> I knew the rules I wanted to follow and wrote down why I bought things. But when
> I owned SPY, QQQ, and NVDA across retirement and taxable accounts, the
> spreadsheet stopped answering the one question that mattered: how much NVDA do I
> actually own? Aerarium is what I built to answer that — and to know when my
> portfolio drifts from my own rules.

This out-performs any feature copy. Use versions of it on LinkedIn, in your IG
bio, in the App Store description, and as your answer whenever someone asks "what
is this?"

## Quick self-check before you post

1. Does it lead with a surprise or a benefit, not a feature name?
2. Is there exactly one idea?
3. Does it avoid every banned word above?
4. Does it tell the reader something true, not tell them what to do?
5. Is the link the UTM-tagged version from `LINKS.csv`?
6. Would a smart, skeptical investor find it credible (not hypey)?

If all six are yes, post it.
