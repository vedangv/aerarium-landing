This policy covers the Aerarium launch page, founder list, and public Research links. The Aerarium Portfolio iOS beta may collect additional app data after you choose to sign in and connect accounts.

  • Email address when you join the founder launch list.

How we use it

  • To send launch updates, beta notes, and founder pricing information.

  • To protect the site from abuse and keep the service reliable.

What this site does not do

  • It does not ask for brokerage credentials.
  • It does not place trades or request trading permissions.

Data controls

To request deletion from the launch list, reply to any Aerarium email or contact the founder directly through the same channel used for beta communication.
